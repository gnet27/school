Greece Postcard

Dear Mr. Bushell,
I am writing to you from Athens, Greece. I’ve been enjoying the amazing food here. Greece has very juicy food the gyro and sovlakia are absolutely amazing! Also, the beaches are very warm, crystal clear and beautiful! Today I went to the Acropolis. It took a very long time going up the the high hill and it was like 90°! I also, went to some museums and noticed that Ancient Greek art is amazing and very creative! I learned that in Greece they don’t mess around about their food! All the food is very rich and tasty! Also, in Greece lots of people run. They have many races in Athens with Greece’s best athletes. Lastly, I noticed that the gods in Greece are very similar from the gods in Rome.
To: Mr. Bushell Room: 221 From: George Papoulidis Photo taken by: George Papoulidis
